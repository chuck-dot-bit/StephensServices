FAQ

Common questions.

Is the audit really free? ▼

Yes — completely free. No email required to view your report. It takes 8 minutes and delivers a personalized analysis instantly. The $2,500 value reflects what a comparable consulting audit would cost.

What's included in the retainer? ▼

36 hours/month: 16 hours of live collaboration (in-office, Zoom, or Meet) and 20 hours of implemented deliverables. These are real assets your team keeps — CRM configurations, documented playbooks, live dashboards, automated workflows.

How is this different from a sales consultant? ▼

Consultants deliver recommendations. This engagement delivers implementations. You don't get a slide deck — you get a configured CRM, a running lead funnel, and documented processes your team can follow after the engagement ends.

What's the $497 strategy session? ▼

A 30-minute paid session where we walk through your audit results together, identify your #1 revenue leak, and build your 90-day roadmap. The $497 is credited toward your first month's retainer if you proceed.

What industries do you work with? ▼

B2B SaaS, manufacturing, HVAC, professional services, distribution, and industrial sectors. The methodology works for any company with a sales team of 1–50 people that sells to other businesses.

Can I cancel the retainer? ▼

Yes — month to month, cancel anytime. The deliverables you've received are yours to keep regardless. The goal is to build systems that outlast the engagement.
